💜 Our Story

Built by Parents,
For Families

As parents with 2 young kids, we know how much the small, simple moments matter. We created Purple Panda so families could have a go-to place to enjoy every day — not just on special occasions.

💜
Why Purple?
During Covid, we rebranded — purple represents brave and courage in gloomy times. Life is not always black & white.
👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Comfort Food Done Right

🎉
Space to Create Memories
